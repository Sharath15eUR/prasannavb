traceroute is a network diagnostic tool used to trace the path that packets take from our device to a destination, such as google.com.

Traceroute Options:

tracert google.com - Trace the path that packet travels from our device to google.com

tracert -h 10 google.com - Shows the trace with maximum of 10 hops .Default is 30 hops

tracert -4 google.com - command that specifically forces IPv4 for the traceroute to google.com.

tracert -6 google.com - command that specifically forces IPv6 for the traceroute to google.com.
